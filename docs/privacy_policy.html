Privacy Policy for Fred's Poker Tracker

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of Freddie Moore ("we," "us," or "our") concerning the mobile application "Fred's Poker Tracker" (the "App").

We have a fundamental commitment to user privacy. The App is designed so that all your data stays entirely on your device — we never see it, collect it, or transmit it anywhere.

2. Our Core Principle: We Do Not Collect Your Personal Data

Fred's Poker Tracker is built to function entirely offline. It does not collect, store on remote servers, transmit, or process any of your personal data.

No User Accounts

No registration, login, or personal information is required to use the App.

No Automated Data Collection

The App does not use analytics software, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or crash reporting tools. We do not automatically collect information such as your IP address, device identifiers, location, or usage patterns.

No Analytics or Tracking — ever:
  • No Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar tools
  • No advertising SDKs or ad identifiers
  • No user behaviour tracking
  • No crash reporting services (Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)
  • No A/B testing or session recording

No Internet Connection Required

The App functions entirely offline and does not make any network requests. No data is ever sent to external servers.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created within the App is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Player profiles: Names and display colours you assign
  • Player groups: Groups you create to organise players
  • Session records: Dates, buy-in amounts, and session outcomes
  • Financial records: Buy-ins, re-buys, cash-out amounts, and settlement calculations

This data:

  • Remains exclusively on your device
  • Is never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App
  • May be included in your iCloud backup if you have device-level iCloud backups enabled — this is controlled entirely by your iOS settings

4. Information You Voluntarily Provide

The only circumstance in which we may receive your personal data is if you contact us for support by email.

Our handling of any support correspondence is governed by the following principles:

  • Purpose limitation: We will use your information solely to respond to your support inquiry.
  • Data minimisation: We will not ask for information beyond what is needed to resolve your issue.
  • No third-party sharing: We will never share your support correspondence with any third party.
  • No marketing: We will not use your email address to send unsolicited communications.
  • Data retention: Support email correspondence is retained for up to 12 months, after which it is permanently deleted.

5. App Permissions

The App does not request any device permissions. It requires no access to:

  • Internet or network
  • Location
  • Camera or photos
  • Contacts
  • Microphone
  • Calendar or reminders
  • Any other device sensors or personal data

6. Data Security

All data is stored locally using iOS's standard secure storage mechanisms provided by SwiftData. Since no data is transmitted over a network, there is no risk of interception in transit.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. As the App collects no personal data at all, we believe it is safe for all audiences. If you believe we have inadvertently received information from a child via a support request, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal data through the App, there is no data for you to access, correct, or delete from our systems.

Data stored locally

All data on your device can be deleted by clearing the App's data in your iOS settings or by uninstalling the App.

Support correspondence

If you have contacted us for support and wish to have your correspondence deleted, email us at travelrate@proton.me and we will delete it within 30 days.

9. Data Protection (UK GDPR)

For users in the United Kingdom and European Economic Area (EEA):

  • Data Controller: Freddie Moore
  • Legal Basis: We do not process personal data through the App. Support correspondence is processed on the basis of legitimate interests (responding to your inquiry).
  • Data Subject Rights: As we collect no data through the App, rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and portability do not apply to App usage. For support correspondence, you may request deletion by contacting us.
  • Data Transfers: The App does not transfer any personal data outside the UK/EEA.
  • Right to Complain: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data protection rights have been violated.
