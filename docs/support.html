Getting Started

Add your players

Go to the Players tab and tap + to add each person you play with. Give them a name and pick a colour — that's all you need.

Organise players into groups

In the Players tab, tap Groups in the segment at the top, then tap + to create a group (e.g. "Wednesday Night"). Assign members to the group so you can add them all to a session in one tap.

Start a session

Tap + on the Sessions tab, set the buy-in amount, then select players or tap Add All next to a group. Tap Start to begin.

During a session

Each player card has a Re-buy button (add more chips) and a Cash Out button (record their final stack). If someone arrives late, tap the ··· menu in the top-right and choose Add Players.

Ending a session

Tap End Session, enter final chip counts for anyone still playing, and confirm. The app will calculate who owes what and show the minimum number of transfers needed to settle up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add a player after the session has started?

Yes. During an active session tap the ··· menu (top-right) and choose Add Players. Select the players you want to add and enter their buy-in amount.

How do I record a re-buy?

Tap the Re-buy button on a player's card during an active session and enter the amount. Each re-buy is tracked individually and added to their total buy-in.

What if the chip counts don't add up?

When ending a session, the app warns you if the total chips cashed out don't match the total pot. You can adjust values before confirming.

How does settlement work?

The app uses a greedy algorithm to calculate the minimum number of transfers needed to settle all balances — so rather than everyone paying everyone, it finds the simplest path.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iOS settings, the app's data will be included in those backups. The app itself makes no network connections.

How do I delete a player?

In the Players tab, swipe left on a player and tap Delete. Note: this also removes all their session history.

How do I delete a group?

In the Players tab, switch to Groups, then swipe left on the group and tap Delete. This only deletes the group — the players themselves are not affected.
